## The Content Production Standard

Every piece produced via this wiki must satisfy **three simultaneous standards:**

1. **[[wildnatedge-writing-standards]]** — brand voice, no em dashes, no filler, no beginner assumptions, active voice, 3–5 line paragraphs
2. **[[content-scoring-rubric]]** — score 75+/100 before publishing (E-E-A-T 25 + Content Quality 20 + Keyword/Intent 15 + LLM Visibility 15 + Page-Type 15 + Technical 10)
3. **[[flesch-reading-ease]] ≥ 50** — target 60–70; absolute minimum 50

## Core Thesis

WildnetEdge content wins by being more helpful, more structured, and more authoritative than competitor content — not by being louder. The [[answer-capsule-framework]] is the structural execution: every section is a self-contained extractable answer serving both the human reader and the AI extraction layer simultaneously.

## Hard Rules (Never Violate)

1. No em dashes in blogs
2. [[flesch-reading-ease]] 50+ on all published content
3. Never assume beginner-level knowledge — audience is decision-makers
4. No generic filler — every section provides original insight or actionable takeaway
5. AI is a drafting partner, not an author — inject expertise, verify claims, improve flow
