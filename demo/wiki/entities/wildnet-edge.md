# WildnetEdge

A technology company operating at the intersection of strategy and execution, with an AI-first positioning. Produces content through this wiki for its website, service pages, blogs, newsletters, social media, and case studies. All content must reinforce WildnetEdge's brand positioning and pass the [[content-scoring-rubric]].

## Brand Positioning (Mandatory in All Content)

- **Strategy + execution intersection** — not a pure consultancy, not a pure dev shop
- **AI-first mindset** — leads with AI solutions, perspectives, and capabilities
- **Business outcomes, not features** — never list features without connecting to business result
- **Compounding selection probability via AI presence** — a specific WildnetEdge value narrative: appearing in AI search = compounding advantage over time
- **Reduced reliance on paid acquisition** — organic + AI-driven growth narrative
- Engages early in **intent-definition stages** — positions as a strategic partner, not a vendor

## Brand Voice

See [[brand-voice-wildnet-edge]] for full tone guide. Summary:
- Conversational, Insightful, Confident, Clear, Human, Practical
- **Avoid:** jargon stacking, buzzword overload, sales-heavy messaging, empty claims, robotic phrasing

## Target Audience

- CTOs, Product Leaders, Digital Heads
- Startup Founders
- Enterprise Stakeholders
- All decision-maker level — **never assume beginner knowledge**

## Content Channels

- Website pages (general): 1,200–1,500 words
- Service pages: 1,500–2,000 words
- Industry pages: 1,400–2,000 words
- Blogs: 1,500–2,000 words
- Newsletters: 800–1,200 words
- Case studies: 500–800 words
- LinkedIn: 20–50 words
- Instagram: 50–150 words
