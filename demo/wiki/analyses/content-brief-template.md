# Content Brief Template

Fill this out and hand it to the wiki agent before requesting a blog, service page, or any content. The more complete the brief, the better the output. Every field maps to a specific scoring requirement in the [[content-scoring-rubric]].

## Brief

**Primary keyword:** (e.g., "hire iOS app developers USA")

**Search intent:** Informational / Commercial / Transactional (see [[keyword-intent-mapping]])
