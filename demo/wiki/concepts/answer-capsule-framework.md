# Answer Capsule Framework

A structural unit of content that Google and LLMs can extract directly for [[ai-overviews-aio]] and [[geo-llm-visibility]]. Every major H2/H3 section of a [[wildnet-edge]] piece should be formatted as an answer capsule. This is the core structural execution of [[e-e-a-t]] and GEO at the sentence level.

## The Five-Part Capsule

1. **Question-form heading (H2 or H3)** — mirrors the user's natural language query: "How do you hire iOS developers?" not "iOS Developer Hiring Overview"
2. **1–2 sentence direct answer immediately beneath** — this is the extract target. Write it as if answering a voice search query. Completeness in 2 sentences.
3. **2–5 supporting bullet points** — evidence, context, nuance, or sub-steps
4. **One cited statistic with named source** — e.g., "Companies using staff augmentation report saving 40–60% vs US full-time hires (WildnetEdge, 2026)"
5. **(Optional) "Why this is true" or "How we know this" paragraph** — depth for readers who want more; also increases E-E-A-T

## Why This Matters

- 44% of AIO citations come from the **first 30% of a page** — put direct answers before context, not after
- Sourced statistics are cited 40% more often by LLMs than opinion-only content (Princeton GEO Study 2024)
- Question-form H2s match the conversational queries used in AI chatbots — the content becomes the exact answer to the question the user asked the AI

## Example (from reference blog)

**H2:** "Tip 6: Verify App Store Compliance Knowledge"

**Direct answer (implied):** Developers working on healthcare, fintech, or e-commerce apps need fluency in HealthKit data handling, ATT compliance, in-app purchase compliance, and privacy nutrition label construction.

**Evidence:** A single App Store rejection resets the launch timeline; in enterprise contexts this can cost significant delayed revenue.

**How to test this (the "Why we know this" layer):** Describe your app's data collection model and ask the candidate to walk through ATT compliance. A developer with real submission experience answers without hesitation.
