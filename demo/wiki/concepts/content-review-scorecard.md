## Definition

The content review scorecard is WildnetEdge's quality gate for all published content. A blog must score **75 or above out of 100** before it is cleared for publishing. A service page minimum is also 75/100.

## Scoring Dimensions

| Dimension | What is checked |
|---|---|
| SEO optimization | Focus keyword placement, secondary keyword distribution, meta description |
| E-E-A-T signals | Author credentials, cited sources, first-hand experience markers |
| Readability | [[flesch-reading-ease]] score ≥ 50 (target 60–70) |
| Content depth | Answer capsule completeness, tip specificity, FAQ coverage |
| Brand voice | No em dashes, American English, no filler, active voice |
| GEO/LLM visibility | Answer capsule format, direct answers, structured data markers |
