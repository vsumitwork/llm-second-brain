## Definition

Staff augmentation is a hiring model where a company adds external developers directly into its existing team on a time-bound or ongoing basis. The external developers report to the client's leads, work inside the client's codebase and processes, and carry no separate delivery overhead.

Contrast with other models:
- **Staff augmentation:** developer integrates into your team; you own architecture and compliance
- **Managed services / agency:** vendor owns delivery and accountability end-to-end
- **Freelance:** unaffiliated, short-term, no Business Associate Agreement possible
