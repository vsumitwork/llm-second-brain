# Keyword Research & Intent Mapping

The framework for matching content type to user intent. **Mismatching content type to intent is one of the most common and costly SEO errors.** Every piece of [[wildnet-edge]] content must be built around a declared intent type.

## Four Intent Types

| Intent | User Goal | Content Format | Commercial Value |
|--------|-----------|---------------|-----------------|
| **Informational** | Learn something | Blog, guide, FAQ, explainer | Low–Medium |
| **Navigational** | Find a specific site | Homepage, login, brand page | Variable |
| **Commercial** | Research before buying | Comparison, review, roundup | High |
| **Transactional** | Buy/act now | Service page, landing page, product page | Very High |

## Funnel Stage Mapping

- **TOFU (Top of Funnel):** Informational keywords — "what is content marketing?", "how to improve SEO." Blogs and educational guides. Goal: awareness and topical authority.
- **MOFU (Middle of Funnel):** Commercial intent — "best content marketing agencies," "software comparison." Comparison posts, pillar pages. Goal: consideration.
- **BOFU (Bottom of Funnel):** Transactional intent — "hire content marketing agency," "pricing." Service pages, case studies. Goal: conversion.

## Keyword Modifier Patterns by Intent

| Modifier Type | Example Modifiers | Intent |
|---|---|---|
| Educational | what is, how to, why, guide, tutorial | Informational |
| Evaluative | best, top, review, vs, comparison, pros/cons | Commercial Investigation |
| Local | near me, in [city], local | Local Transactional |
| Temporal | 2026, latest, new, updated | Freshness-sensitive Informational |
| Purchase-ready | buy, hire, pricing, cost, quote, agency | Transactional |
| Problem-aware | fix, solve, improve, increase, reduce | Commercial/Informational |

## Keyword Prioritisation Matrix

| Factor | Weight | Notes |
|--------|--------|-------|
| Business Relevance | 30% | How directly does this relate to a WildnetEdge service? |
| Keyword Difficulty (KD) | 25% | KD 0–30 = 10/10; KD 31–60 = 6/10; KD 61–100 = 2/10 |
| Search Volume | 20% | > 1,000/mo = 10/10; 100–999 = 5/10 |
| Commercial Intent | 15% | Transactional = 10/10; Commercial = 7/10; Informational = 4/10 |
| AIO/LLM Opportunity | 10% | Long-tail informational queries = highest AIO opportunity |

## Long-Tail Strategy for AIO

Long-tail keywords (7+ words) trigger [[ai-overviews-aio]] 65.9% of the time (DataSlayer 2025). Strategy:
- Build long-tail clusters around each service offering
- Use "People Also Ask" questions from search results — direct long-tail targets
- Include question-form long-tails in H2/H3 headings
- Create FAQ sections targeting top 5–8 long-tail questions per topic

## [[keyword-density-targets]] (from [[wildnatedge-writing-standards]])

- Primary keyword: 6–10 uses per 1,000 words (0.8–1.2%; max 1.5%)
- Secondary: 2–4 uses each
- [[keyword-placement-priority]]: H1, first 100 words, at least one H2, meta title, meta description, URL slug, conclusion
